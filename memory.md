# Memory Items
Epinephrine dose for ACLS:
- 1mg q3-5 min for adults (0.01mg/kg for children)

Seizure dose:
- Lorazepam 4mg SL/IV (0.1mg/kg)
- Midazolam 5mg IM (0.2mg/kg) IM/IV
  
Status Epilepticus (>5 minutes):
- Levetiracetam (20 mg/kg - q12h or sooner)
- SLOW Phenytoin / Dilantin (15-20mg/kg IV - q12h or sooner - ensure not pregnant - infuse over 10-20 minutes)

Bradycardia:
- Atropine 1mg IV bolus

Arrythmia:
- Amiodarone 300 mg IV bolus

SVT:
- Adenosine 6 mg IV bolus

Nitro medications are contraindicated in right-sided or inferior myocardial infarction
