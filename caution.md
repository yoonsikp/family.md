# Risks, Side effects, and Harms

## Medications

### Ibuprofen (Advil)
- Acute kidney injury
- Stomach ulcers

### Paracetamol (Tylenol)
- Liver toxicity

### Acetylsalicylic Acid (Aspirin)
- Reye's syndrome in children
- Overdose
- Bleeding

### Proton Pump Inhibitors
- Bone fracture (long term)
- Magnesium deficiency

### Statins
- Muscle pain/injury
- Liver toxicity

### Beta blockers
- Sexual dysfunction
- Asthma exacerbation
- Hyperkalemia

### Lasix or Thiazide Diuretics
- Hypokalemia
- Hyponatremia
- Acute kidney injury

### ACE Inhibitors
- Cough
- Hyperkalemia
- Acute kidney injury

### ARBs
- Hyperkalemia

### Spironolactone
- Hyperkalemia
- Gynecomastia

### Cephalosporins
- Alcohol interaction (toxicity)
- C. diff infection

### Amoxicillin-clavulanate
- Liver toxicity
- Diarrhea
- C. diff infection

### Warfarin
- Alcohol interaction (bleeding)

### Nitroglycerin
- PDE5 inhibitors contraindicated

### TMP/SMX & Metronidazole
- Alcohol interaction (toxicity)

### Methotrexate
- Liver toxicity
- Kidney Toxicity
- Respiratory symptoms
- Cytopenias

### Azathioprine
- Liver injury

### Empagliflozin
- Urinary tract infection
- Foot ulcers

### Terbinafine
- Liver injury

### Haloperidol
- Contraindicated in dementia and Parkinson's
- EPS
- Elevated prolactin

### Risperidone and other Antipsychotics
- EPS
- Metabolic syndrome
- Elevated prolactin

### Lithium
- Renal insufficiency
- Liver toxicity
- Hypothyroidism
- Overdose
- NSAID interactions

### Valproate
- Overdose
- Liver toxicity

### Phenytoin (Dilantin)
- Overdose
- Liver toxicity
- Anemia
- Skin rash
- Toxic epidermal necrolysis

### Lamotrigine
- Anemia
- Skin rash
- Toxic epidermal necrolysis

### Fluoroquinolone
- Achilles rupture
- Tendinopathy
- C. diff infection

### Corticosteroid
- Weight gain
- Osteoporosis (long term)
- Stomach ulcers
- Glaucoma
- Cataracts

### Digoxin
- Overdose
- GI Upset
- Hyperkalemia

### Salbutamol
- Hypokalemia

### Amiodarone
- Pulmonary toxicity
- Thyroid disease
- Liver toxicity
- Blue man syndrome

### PrEP (Truvada)
- Kidney damage

### SSRI
- Serotonin syndrome
- Sexual dysfunction
- Hyponatremia
- Mania/suicide

### Tricyclic antidepressant
- Serotonin syndrome
- Overdose
- Restlessness

### Bupropion (Wellbutrin)
- Seizure risk

### Trazadone
- Priapism

### Benzodiazepines
- Alcohol interaction (death)
- Addiction
- Delirium

### Opioids
- Alcohol interaction (death)
- Addiction

### Z-drugs
- Fall risk
- Sleepwalking

### Blood Transfusion
- Alloimmunization (1/100)
- Allergic reaction (1/100)
- Transfusion associated circulatory overload (TACO) (1/1000)
- Lung injury (1/10000)
- Viral infection (1/100000)

## Vaccines

### Rotavirus Oral (Rotarix)
- Intussusception (1/10000)

### COVID-19 mRNA (BioNTech)
- Myocarditis (1/10000)

## Procedures
### Surgery
- Infection
- Intraoperative injury
- Bleeding
- Blood clots
- Anesthesia complications
- Vocal cord injury/paralysis

### Cervical Manipulation
- Vertebral artery dissection (1/10000)

## Investigations
### MRI Scan
- Metal shards
- Hearing loss (without ear protection)

### CT Scan
- Cancer risk (1/1000)
- Acute Kidney Injury from Contrast
