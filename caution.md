# Risks, Side effects, and Harms

## Medications

## Warfarin
- Alcohol interaction (bleeding)

## TMP/SMX & Metronidazole
- Alcohol interaction (hangover)

### Methotrexate
- Liver toxicity
- Kidney Toxicity
- Respiratory symptoms
- Cytopenias

## Azathioprine
- Liver injury

### Risperidone or Antipsychotic
- EPS
- Sleepiness
- Weight gain
- NMS

### Lithium
- Renal insufficiency
- Liver toxicity
- Intoxication
- Hypothyroidism

### PPI
- Bone fracture (long term)
- Magnesium deficiency

### Advil
- Acute kidney injury

### Tylenol
- Liver toxicity

### Valproate
- Liver toxicity

### Corticosteroid
- Weight gain
- Osteoporosis (long term)
